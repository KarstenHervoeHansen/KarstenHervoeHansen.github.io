DK-Technologies has changed

March 2018. The world is changing and DK-Technologies together with DK-Audio has changed too so the brands we have protected over almost 31 years will survive to the benefit of our loyal customers. However, we have been forced to say goodbye to a lot of legacy products and concentrate on software development and sales for the never families like the DK1 to 5 range and the DKT7. Upon strong interest from customers support of the popular MSD600M++ family could be included. All legacy product documentation and software will be available on this site over time based on volunteer work so donations for this work are highly appreciated. The best regards Karsten Hansen

Mission Statement

DK-Technologies A/S, founded in 1987 as DK-Audio, is a leading worldwide manufacturer of audio and video solutions for the broadcast and post production markets where customer satisfaction and return on investment is paramount. Our sales are handled through a highly trained network of distributors and affiliates, all of whom share our desire for excellence. At DK, we combine a highly educated staff with state-of-the-art technology and intense research into product design, which allows us to develop innovative systems and products that are manufactured to the highest possible standards. Our on-going commitment to quality and service is backed by intense focus on our customers requirements, enabling us to meet our own ambitious standards of excellence.
